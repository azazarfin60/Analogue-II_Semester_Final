# 05: Feedback Concepts

## All About Electronics
The "All About Electronics" playlist covers feedback amplifiers, positive and negative feedback, topologies, and details on each topology.
- **Introduction to Feedback Amplifier**: The concept of feedback, positive vs negative feedback, and the advantages of negative feedback (gain desensitization, reduction of non-linear distortion) are explained in "Introduction to Feedback Amplifier" starting at [0:10](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=__8f6AXenYo&t=10s).
- **Feedback Topologies**: The classification of the four topologies (voltage series, voltage shunt, current series, current shunt) along with output sampling (sensing) and input mixing connections is introduced in "Feedback Topologies in Amplifier Explained" starting at [0:10](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OoNluE5RLNg&t=10s).

## Ankit Goyal Sir
Ankit Goyal Sir provides a rigorous theoretical and network-oriented discussion of feedback:
- **Feedback Basics & Classification**: Introduces feedback loops, forward path amplifiers, feedback paths, comparator/mixer, sampler, and topology identification in "Feedback Amplifiers | Part-1" starting at [2:02](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a8p0d3-b3Lg&t=122s).
- **Feedback Topologies Analysis**: Specifically covers the Shunt-Shunt (voltage shunt) feedback, trans-resistance amplifier models, and effects on input/output resistance in "Feedback Amplifiers | Part-2" starting at [2:32](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yNgqhsEAAnE&t=152s).
