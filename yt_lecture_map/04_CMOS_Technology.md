# 04: CMOS Technology

## All About Electronics
The "All About Electronics" playlist does not contain a dedicated tutorial on CMOS technology or the CMOS Inverter. Basic descriptions of CMOS as a complementary technology can be found in the general discussion of FET types:
- **General FET Types**: Types of FETs including basic introductions to IGFET (which is what MOSFETs and CMOS are based on) are discussed in "What is Field Effect Transistor (FET)?" starting at [3:16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cOICDYuY-gA&t=196s).

## Ankit Goyal Sir
Ankit Goyal Sir covers CMOS technology from an analog standpoint in detail:
- **CMOS Inverter**: Explains the working, design, logic symbol, and transistors involved in constructing a CMOS Inverter. An analogy is made using a simple common-emitter BJT inverter before diving into the CMOS structure in "CMOS Inverter" starting at [1:57](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Fdwhr8slhZY&t=117s).
