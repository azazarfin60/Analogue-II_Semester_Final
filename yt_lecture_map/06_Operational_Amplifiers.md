# 06: Operational Amplifiers

## All About Electronics
All About Electronics has a highly comprehensive collection on Operational Amplifiers (Op-Amps) covering both applications and practical limits:
- **Basics & Ideal Op-Amp**: Introduction to operational amplifiers and ideal characteristics is covered in "Introduction to Operational Amplifier: Characteristics of Ideal Op-Amp" starting at [0:16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kiiA6WTCQn0&t=16s).
- **Inverting Amplifier & Virtual Ground**: Virtual ground concept and inverting amplifiers are explained in "Operational Amplifier: Inverting Op Amp" starting at [0:16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AuZ00cQ0UrE&t=16s).
- **Non-Inverting & Buffer**: Non-inverting configuration and buffer applications are taught in "Operational Amplifier: Non-Inverting Op-Amp" starting at [0:16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uyOfonR_rEw&t=16s).
- **Integrator**: Integral functions of Op-Amps are analyzed in "Op-Amp Integrator" starting at [0:16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OPvs7A554Rw&t=16s).
- **Differentiator**: Derivative functions are covered in "Op-Amp Differentiator" starting at [0:16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aU24RWIgJVs&t=16s).
- **Instrumentation Amplifier**: Design, derivation of the differential voltage gain ($A_d$), and noise-cancellation advantages of the 3-op-amp instrumentation amplifier are explained in "Instrumentation Amplifier Explained (with Derivation)" starting at [0:15](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pSctPegtZfc&t=15s).
- **Practical Limitations**:
  - **Slew Rate**: Slew rate concept and examples are analyzed in "Op-Amp Slew Rate Explained" starting at [0:16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2DFIr6t1hbc&t=16s).
  - **CMRR**: Common Mode Rejection Ratio is covered in "Op-Amp: CMRR" starting at [0:16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpCu3HbAiWg&t=16s).
  - **Input Offset Voltage**: Offset voltage is covered in "Op-Amp: Input Offset Voltage Explained" starting at [0:16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hUu3SqRSYyA&t=16s).
  - **Input Bias & Offset Currents**: Bias and offset currents are covered in "Op-Amp: Input Bias Current and Input Offset Current Explained" starting at [0:16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c3X7_ohCTSw&t=16s).

## Ankit Goyal Sir
Ankit Goyal Sir covers Op-Amp basics, practical features, negative feedback applications, and detailed network derivations:
- **Op-Amp Basics & Ideal Parameters**: Differential gain, virtual short, and basic concepts are introduced in "Basics of Operational Amplifier" starting at [3:40](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WrW3br8zC1U&t=220s).
- **Op-Amp Characteristics & Limits**: Detailed analysis of Input Offset Voltage starting at [48:27](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=acc99d_0OCc&t=2907s) and Slew Rate starting at [92:47](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=acc99d_0OCc&t=5567s).
- **Op-Amp Integrator & Differentiator**: Coverage of ideal and practical integrators and differentiators in "Negative Feedback Applications Part - 02" starting at [33:34](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KRwDIwmbkv4&t=2014s).
- **Instrumentation Amplifier**: Mathematical model derivations of the 3-op-amp instrumentation amplifier, including transducer interface applications (Wheatstone bridge setups) and common-mode signal rejection analysis are derived in "Negative Feedback Applications Part - 02" starting at [08:02](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KRwDIwmbkv4&t=482s).
